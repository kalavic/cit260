Lesson 02

Communication is really important. If you have to make a schedule change for a meeting or if we need to set a time to meet, please do it early rather than a few hours beforehand. It makes it really difficult to coordinate and get things done when we are all depending on one another if there is a lack of communication. And since this is an online class and our collaboration is all based on virtual chats and emailing it's even more important that everyone stay on top of the communication and coordination of meetings.

Communication is something that I’m really worried about for this class because I can’t be flexible with my schedule unless I’m told ahead of time what my team needs and wants to see happen during the week. I hope that things work out and the semester will go smoothly.
